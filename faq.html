Frequently Asked Questions

Documents • Insurance • Deposits • Delivery • Age • Cross‑border

What documents are required?

Valid driver’s license, passport/ID, and a credit card for the security deposit. International Driving Permit may be required depending on license country.

What about insurance and deposits?

All vehicles are fully insured. A refundable deposit is held based on the model. Excess and coverage details are provided with your quote and contract.

Can you deliver to my hotel, airport or villa?

Yes. We offer concierge delivery and collection across Europe, including hotels, airports and private residences.

Do prices change by season?

Yes, rates vary by high/low season, location and availability. We confirm an all‑inclusive quote before booking.

Minimum driver age?

Typically 25+ for supercars. Some SUVs may be available from 23+, subject to confirmation.

Can I cross borders?

Yes, with prior approval. Please share your route in advance so we can confirm coverage.
