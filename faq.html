What’s included in the price?

Basic insurance, mileage as per offer, and assistance. Extras: out-of-city delivery, additional mileage, extra driver.

Do you deliver to hotels or airports?

Yes — hotel, airport and private residence deliveries by appointment.

Are prices seasonal?

Yes. Rates vary with season, demand, delivery distance and availability.
